### What is a Perp DEX?

A **perpetual futures decentralized exchange (perp DEX)** is an on-chain venue for trading perpetual futures — leveraged contracts that track an asset's price without an expiry date. Unlike spot DEXs, perp DEXs settle PnL in stablecoins, charge funding rates to keep mark price tethered to oracle/index price, and let traders go long or short with leverage (typically 5x–250x, sometimes more).

The two dominant architectures are:

- **CLOB (Central Limit Order Book)** — Hyperliquid, dYdX, Lighter, edgeX, Paradex, Drift. Best for tight spreads and price discovery.
- **Oracle / Pool-based AMMs** — GMX, Jupiter Perps, Gains Network, Avantis. Trades execute against a liquidity pool at oracle price; no slippage on large orders, but capacity is bounded by the pool.

   - The DEX must be live (or in public testnet — mark 🟡)
